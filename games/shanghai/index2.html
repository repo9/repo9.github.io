Player choose a pair of identical tiles to connect, and the connection path must not exceed two turns. If the requirements are met, two tiles will be eliminated.

The player's task is to eliminate all the tiles on the board.

The game is over if future moves are not possible and tiles are left on the board, or the player successfully removes all the tiles.
